Our Story

At ESC Wear, we believe that sportswear should empower you to feel confident, comfortable, and true to yourself. Our journey started with Sohyla Sameh, an ambitious and purpose-driven woman with a vision to create more than just a brand — she wanted to create a movement.

Sohyla has always been passionate about wellness, fitness, and maintaining a balanced lifestyle. Pilates, yoga, meditation, and padel game with her friends were key parts of her daily routine. However, during one of these games, she noticed something: she wasn't feeling confident or comfortable in her workout clothes. She realized that this was not just a personal issue — it was a gap in the market. Modest wear was either stylish but uncomfortable, or functional but lacking in design. Many options weren't long enough, or didn't offer the comfort needed for active sports. Sohyla saw this as an opportunity to create something different. With a deep passion for both business and wellness, she set out to bridge the gap. ESC Wear was born: an Edgy, sporty, and comfy brand designed for the modern modest woman, where performance meets style, and every woman feels seen and included. ESC Wear is not just for hijabis; it's for every woman who values comfort, style, and the freedom to express herself. From our colorful oversized sets to breathable fabrics, and functional pieces like sports bras, hip covers, hijab sets, and one-of-a-kind t-shirts and Abaya sets, we offer a wide variety of stylish, high-performance sportswear for any activity — from jogging and yoga to padel and tennis. We believe that great design and innovative fabrics should not be mutually exclusive. That's why we use light, breathable, moisture-wicking fabrics that ensure you perform your best while feeling confident, comfortable, and empowered.

ESC is more than just sportswear brand — it's about creating community. The ESC Movement Community is a space for every woman to connect, share, and support each other, whether you're a fitness enthusiast or just beginning your journey toward health and wellness. It's a place where you can join running programs, exchange tips, and feel the power of inclusion.

ESC isn't just what you wear. It's how you move, how you feel, and how you show up for yourself — boldly, unapologetically, and in full motion.
